Featured Ingredients

Mulberry Root Extract

Brightens

This potent root native to Japan helps to brighten and even out skin tone by inhibiting the enzyme tyrosinase, which triggers uneven pigment production.

Hyaluronic Acid

Nourishes and moisturizes

This easily absorbed nutrient and antioxidant helps to boost long-lasting moisture and revitalizes the skin’s outer layers.

Olive Fruit Oil

Antioxidant, moisturizes skin

Rich in antioxidants, olive oil maintains the skin’s moisture barrier and helps fight damage caused by free radicals without clogging pores. Our oil is sourced from an organic olive farm in southern Spain.

Olive Leaf Extract

Antioxidant, hydrating

The leaf of the olive tree helps protect the skin against pollution and helps to fight the visible signs of aging.

Arnica Flower Extract

Soothes and conditions skin

Arnica has long been used to soothe irritated skin and moisturize dry skin, and it possesses powerful anti-inflammatory properties.

Perilla Leaf Extract

Restores softness and fights inflammation

Also called Japanese mint and ranging from bright green to deep purple, this leaf boasts antioxidant and anti-inflammatory properties, and increases the skin’s hydration.

Hydrolyzed Collagen

Protects and moisturizes

Skin-plumping collagen is processed into ultra-tiny peptides, making it more easily absorbed and leaving skin feeling soft and supple.

Citric Acid

Exfoliates, brightens

Helps promote pH balance and gently exfoliates skin so that beneficial ingredients can be absorbed. This alpha-hydroxy acid also brightens the skin’s appearance.

Vitamin E

Antioxidant, skin conditioner

Helps prevent the signs of premature aging by protecting skin against environmental pollution and free radicals, and helps other beneficial ingredients absorb into the skin more efficiently.

Japanese Angelica Root Extract

Conditions and moisturizes

This Japanese herb from the carrot family has been clinically proven to protect skin from the effects of photo-aging in a 2017 study at Keimyung University in South Korea.
